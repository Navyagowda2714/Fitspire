## Overview

Fitspyre ("the App") is an AI-powered fitness coaching app for iOS.
We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your health data secure.

## Data Collection

**Fitspyre does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.**

The app stores workout history, rep counts, form scores and streaks locally on your device.
This data stays on your device and is never uploaded to external servers.

## Camera

Fitspyre uses your iPhone camera solely for real-time body pose detection and exercise form analysis. No video, photos or images are ever recorded, stored, or transmitted outside your device. All processing happens entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework.

## Health Data

With your permission, Fitspyre reads health data including steps, active calories and heart rate from Apple Health. This data is only displayed within the app and is never shared with third parties.

## iCloud

Workout data may sync between your own devices via iCloud CloudKit. This sync is entirely within your Apple account and is not accessible to us.

## Sign in with Apple

We use Sign in with Apple for secure authentication. We only receive an anonymised user identifier. No email address or personal details are shared with us unless you explicitly choose to share them.

## Apple Watch

Fitspyre communicates with a paired Apple Watch via WatchConnectivity to deliver haptic form alerts during workouts. No data is stored on the Watch beyond the current session.

## Third-Party Services

Fitspyre does not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or profiling tools. We do not share any data with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

Fitspyre is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Data Security

All app data is stored locally on your device. You control access through your device security settings such as Face ID or Touch ID.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
